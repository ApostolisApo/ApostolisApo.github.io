Currently, I am employed by Etraveli Group and help develop their various iOS apps. Earlier, I have worked as a mobile developer for the consulting companies TietoEVRY and Semcon. I started my career at Talkamatic as a software designer with specialty in dialogue systems. Apart from mobile systems I have experience from backend design (mainly in Python) and web frontend design (JS + Angular). At a personal level, I sell and maintain KnowTrail for iOS and watchOS and KnowDate for iOS. You can click on the links and download them on your iPhone and/or Apple Watch! My academic background is linguistics and computational linguistics and has allowed me to have a more interdisciplinary approach to the art of creating applications and interfaces. Above all, what drives and defines me is a will to create user-friendly and practical applications that allow the user to focus on the task and not on how to beat the interface.
